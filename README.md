# Mo' Cookies

Yeah I know mods by this name already exist, I don't care. Internally `mocookies`.

> [!TIP]  
> Made with love and [Fabric](https://fabricmc.net/wiki/tutorial:start).

## Content

Currently there are:

- Chocolate Chip Cookies (the Minecraft classic)
- Sugar Cookies
- Chocolate Cookies
- Half Moon Cookies
